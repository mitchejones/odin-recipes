Cheeseburger

A delicious looking burger. Valeria Boltneva/Pexels

Description

A quick, easy and cheap burger recipe that will leave you wanting more.

Great for gatherings when you want to grill some nice food. A nice alternative to the rising cost of fast food!

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 pounds of grount beef
  • cheddar cheese slices
  • salt
  • pepper
  • lettuce (optional)
  • tomatoes (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat up your outdoor grill of choice
  2. Add salt and pepper mixture to a large bowl
  3. Add ground beef to bowl
  4. Mash ground beef in the bowl, ensure seasoning well mixed
  5. Grab a large chunk of the mixture and roll into a circle
  6. Flatten the ball of hamburger, place on grill
  7. Let cook for a few minutes
  8. Before taking the patty off the grill, place a cheese slice on top of it to melt
  9. Place burger on a hamburger bun, add preferred toppings and enjoy!
