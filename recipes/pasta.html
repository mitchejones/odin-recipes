Quick Pasta

Mostaccioli pasta with tomato sauce. Engin Akyurt/Pexels

Description

A cheap and easy pasta recipe.

Great for the ordinary lunch or dinner with the family.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of mostaccioli or any type of pasta
  • 1 jar of canned pasta sauce
  • salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a medium pot with water and heat until boiling
  2. Add salt to taste
  3. Add 1 or more cups of pasta to pot
  4. Cook pasta for 7-9 minutes
  5. Drain water from pot
  6. Add pasta to plate
  7. Add 1 serving of pasta sauce to dish. Enjoy!
